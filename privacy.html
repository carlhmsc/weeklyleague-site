Privacy Policy

WeeklyLeague values your privacy. This app stores only minimal data necessary to function:

  • TikTok access and refresh tokens (to authenticate uploads)
  • Video metadata (title, link, view count)
  • Upload history to avoid duplicates

No personal information beyond what TikTok provides via its API is collected or shared.

To revoke access or request deletion of data, contact: weeklyleague@example.com.
